Know your enemy

You should always expect your enemy to do tasks, and to stay at a distance. To counter this make sure to stay in places where you can perform an easy kill. As sun tzu once said: "Keep your friends close, but keep your enemies closer."

Be unpredictable

Always go into directions people won't usually go to, also don't forget to fake tasks.
